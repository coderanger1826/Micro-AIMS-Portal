The Micro AIMS Portal is designed to simplify academic operations for institutions. It ensures transparency, efficiency, and collaboration between Students, Instructors, Faculty Advisors, and Admins. By centralizing user registration, course management, and academic monitoring, the portal enables institutions to modernize their workflows and reduce manual dependencies.

Students can register and apply for course enrollment directly through the platform. The portal keeps students informed of their enrollment status via real-time updates. With personalized dashboards, students can view their academic progress, manage their course schedules, and receive important notifications seamlessly.

Faculty Advisors and Instructors can verify enrollment requests, review academic records, and provide feedback to students through their dedicated dashboards. Faculty Advisors, with their enhanced tools, can mentor students effectively by tracking their academic performance and offering personalized guidance. Instructors can also collaborate with Faculty Advisors to align academic goals and ensure the success of every student.
